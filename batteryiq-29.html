The Problem

Battery intelligence shouldn't be locked behind proprietary OEM data.

Every electric vehicle generates massive amounts of operational data, yet the battery degradation models that explain battery health remain proprietary to manufacturers. Fleet operators are forced to make expensive maintenance and replacement decisions without transparent battery intelligence.

🔒

Proprietary OEM Battery Models

Battery degradation curves and health algorithms remain inaccessible to fleet owners and independent operators.

📉

Limited Battery Visibility

Without independent battery intelligence, fleets struggle to understand true battery condition and remaining useful life.

💰

Expensive Decision Making

Early battery replacements waste money. Late replacements increase downtime and operational risk.

⚠

Reactive Maintenance

Most fleet platforms only report current battery status instead of predicting future degradation.

The Solution

Meet BatteryIQ

BatteryIQ combines telematics, charging behaviour, environmental conditions, and machine learning to independently estimate battery health and degradation — providing actionable insights without requiring proprietary OEM models.
